OUR MISSION

To nurture and mould the child to be responsive to global dynamics through effective teaching and learning within value system of excellence, honesty, discipline and hard work.

VISION

To make Vision Academy a centre of excellence in provision of holistic education for nurturing lifelong dreams at a young age.

OUR CORE VALUE

To nurture and develop meaningful relationship with the community. Our staff is committed to providing an environment that nurtures personal growth and self-esteem. All members of the school community have rights and responsibilities and these are best ensured when agreed procedures are accepted and followed. Vision Academy children are developing appropriate ways to interact with others. The school and its community share the responsibility to assist the kids in learning appropriate behaviours.

STRATEGIC PLAN

Provides a system of support to ensure instructions and enrichments meet the needs of all children
